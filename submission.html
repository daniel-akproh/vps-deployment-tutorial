Capture Your Best Selfie

  1. Wear your regular makeup and hairstyle, keeping hair away from your brows and no glasses.
  2. Using a selfie stick, take a headshot only—head and shoulders with clean background excluding ceiling or other room parts.
  3. As many phones automatically have filters, you'll need to check your camera settings and remove them.
  4. Stand facing a window (not in direct sunlight to avoid hot spots, and shadows), ensuring decent pixelation.
  5. Keep your chin parallel to the floor, face straight ahead with no angles, and position the camera lens at eye level, looking directly into it.
  6. Show a pleasant expression, smiling slightly with both your eyes and mouth.
